An organization focused on support for the post-war recovery of IT in Ukraine
Our Charter

To become a source of information and a

place to meet people who are interested

in helping Ukrainian IT on Day One after

the war's end.

Charter Team members will contribute

their experience working with the

UA IT industry during the Russian

Invasion and after.

Our Plan

  • Create and publish interviews with

    interesting people willing to share

    heir experience

  • Create and share posts, monitor

    activities

  • Make available links to other

    published materials that provide

    information about UA IT

Our Mission

  • Recruit SMEs in Verticals

  • Publish White Papers, Podcast

  • Establish Regular Meetings and

    discuss roadblocks, challenges

  • Find forums to bring positive news

    about UA IT
